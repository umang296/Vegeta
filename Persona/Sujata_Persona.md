## Shopping Behaviour

Although, she is a shopaholic, she is cautious with products she buys, she searches for detailed descriptions and reviews before she purchases anything as she has gotten scammed a few times before. She relies on reviews a lot so she prefers if the website has section where people leave reviews that can be viewed by other buyers.


---

## Problems With Online Shopping

She is frustrated by the online shopping experiences these days as she finds websites complicated and slow. She can’t navigate or find the product she is looking for easily. She also does not like that there are no proper shipping options, most have one shipping option with expensive rates. The main problem she has is while checking out, the websites lag and are very slow making the checkout frustrating and anxious as all bank details are filled out. She has been in a situation before where the checkout process lagged making her payment go through twice. She has also been scammed as she did not get the same product as the picture on the website, so she appreciates detailed descriptions and reviews and does not rely on just the picture.

Emily prefers websites that offer:

She is at a lookout for a website where she can find all products for her projects, needs and wants with affordable product and shipping rates with no hidden cost and simple checkout process that feels safe and makes her shopping experience enjoyable not a frustrating and anxious process.
